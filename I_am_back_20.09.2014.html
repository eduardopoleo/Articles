I finally made my made for Tealeaf Academy because (imo) they offer the best (content depth & quality) to (price ratio). Also the fact that it is an online course made it very convenient for me at this point. The course is basically divided in 3 modules described as follows:

INTRODUCTION TO RUBY AND WEB DEVELOPMENT 4 Weeks $475 US
RAPID PROTOTYPING WITH RUBY ON RAILS 4 Weeks $610 US
BUILD ROBUST AND PRODUCTION QUALITY APPLICATIONS 8 weeks $1485

Another nice thing about this course is that you do not have to paid the full fee upfront, instead you may decide to go module by module. You can also skip any module you consider unnecessary for you. I have only seen few other courses that offer similar payment flexibility, (maybe Thinkful or Udacity with its upcoming nanodegrees).

As I mentioned before the course is very complete, it goes all the way from ruby basics to advance development techniques in rails such as, TDD, best practices, etc.

I already finished their first course and I enjoyed it a lot. The course helped me getting started in ruby syntax, OOP, and basics of web development. I found it incredibly useful even with the fact that I already had some previous programing experience.
